The rules

The three users that make more suscsefull clasifications between the day 4 of october at 0 GMT and the day 11 of october 0 GMT will win (at least one of the Winners have to be a Woman).

What is a successful classification?

We will consider a successful classification when you really can identify the city and there are enough data to confirm it's the real target. The "I don't know" will not take on account. But every 5 "I don't know" will rest one score to the final score. Incase of two users have thse same score, one with less "I don't know" will win.

What is the prize?

The price consist on a Revel Model of the ISS, a 76/300 dobson telescope and a LEGO Research institute.

The winners will choose according to their score.
